For Merchants

Provide Incredible Customer Experiences, Earn Incremental Revenue

White-Labeled Claims Management

Provide a fully customizable claims portal for your customers that allows them to file claims, retrieve e-receipts, coordinate repair from local service providers or get paid quickly. Zinc runs on top of your existing support framework (e.g. ZenDesk integrations).

Formation and Compliance

Zinc works with insurance carriers, banks and other stakeholders to expedite the process of offering warranty or protection insurance at point of sale. Forget the multi-month process - Zinc makes onboarding lightening fast.

Online Sales + Offline Repairs

Tap into local repair and service providers to allow your consumers to get on-demand servicing they need without the paperwork and hassle they hate. Zinc can also integrate into your own service network and help you eliminate paperwork and duplicative data entry.

Powerful Data Analytics Tools

Use Zinc analytics to better understand the warranty and protection needs of your customers. Offer a different mix of plans, fine tune deductibles, control liability limits and strike partnerships with repair providers. Zinc makes it easy to optimize a warranty and protection plan tailored for you.

For Developers

A Complete Insurance Stack, Made Easy for Developers

Clean API tools that allow developers to quickly deploy the infrastructure behind providing elegant insurance experiences for consumers. Dynamic in-line “insure it” buttons at checkout with complete control on how pricing and options are displayed. Zinc integrates with your existing payments, support and checkout frameworks to seamlessly enroll your customers for warranty and protection insurance.
Scalable and orthogonal APIs that connect to a fully customizable claims platform that grows with your business. Beautiful interfaces and abstractions to help you manage receipts, policy renewals, insurance claims files, repair orders, payments to service providers and regulatory reports. For Consumers

Protect and Fix the Things You Love

Zinc connects the online world with offline service providers to provide a revolutionary experience for millions of consumers. No more receipts, no more hotlines, no more clunky administrative procedures – get your things protected and fixed with ease. Find local service providers – seamstresses, repairmen and others – and let Zinc help you setup an appointment, handle your claims filing and get your repair or servicing paid instantly.


If you lost an item or it can’t be fixed – let Zinc handle getting you a replacement. Forgot where you bought that lamp or jacket from? Zinc allows you to access the claims portals of participating merchants through an easy to use mobile and desktop platform. Need to speak to a specialist? Zinc connects you directly to the merchants support line. This is insurance reimagined – this is Zinc.

Build relationships with merchants, strike partnerships with retailers and grow your business – Zinc empowers you to reach new heights.
